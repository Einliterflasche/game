I have always enjoyed Harry Potter style spell-casting.

I want to play a fast paced multiplayer pvp game where I can cast spells,
try to dogde or defend against enemy spells.

Endgame: MMO Battle of Hogwarts style campaigns, along with open world (incl. PvE) for equipment gathering etc.

For now: 1v1 in an colosseum style arena

# Combat

Inspired by Harry Potter, so not just elemental spells.

All players + mobs have a health bar, that is deducted from if they take damage
i.e. from a fire bolt.

## Spells

 - stun bolt
 - generic shield spell
   - Forms an elliptic shield infront of the character, blue haze
   - Disappears after some time (very fast, like 1 second? Or maybe after some more time...)
   - Protects against bolt spells at least, breakable by shield breaker
 - fire bolt
 - shield breaker spells
 - healing spell

Interactions should be "realistic".
